Structural Design Review Services

Our primary focus is independent structural design review for municipalities, architects, and project teams working on complex or high-risk buildings.

Review Services

  • Permit review support for cities and municipalities
  • Independent peer review for architects and owners
  • Complex system and load path evaluation
  • Connection and constructability review
  • Identification of common design and detailing risks

Approach

We combine traditional structural review with targeted input from a broader network of experienced engineers. This allows us to identify issues earlier, improve consistency, and focus attention on the elements that most impact safety and performance.

Design Services (by request)

While our focus is review, select structural design services may be provided under separate scope and applicable licensure when appropriate.
